# 🌐 JGITeC2025 - 1st Joint Global Innovation and Technology Conference

Welcome to the official repository for the **1st Joint Global Innovation and Technology Conference 2025 (JGITeC2025)** landing page. This event aims to bridge the disciplines of engineering, business, and pharmaceutical sciences to foster global innovation.

## 📍 Event Details

- **Event Name**: Joint Global Innovation and Technology Conference 2025 (JGITeC2025)  
- **Date**: October 25, 2025  
- **Location**: Jakarta Global University Ballroom  
- **Theme**: Bridging engineering, business, and pharmaceutical expertise to advance technological innovation for global impact

---

## 💡 About This Project

This landing page was built using **HTML5**, **Bootstrap 5**, and custom CSS to match the red theme of **Jakarta Global University (JGU)**. It is fully responsive and designed to present the key highlights of the event, including:

- Keynote Speakers  
- Editorial Board  
- Conference Topics  
- Venue and Date  
- Contact Information

---

## 🎯 Topics Covered at the Conference

### 🔧 Engineering
- Informatic Engineering
- Industrial Engineering
- Civil Engineering
- Electrical Engineering
- Mechanical Engineering

### 💊 Pharmaceutical Science
- Drug Design, Herbal Technology, Pharmacology, Biotechnology

### 💼 Business and Entrepreneurship
